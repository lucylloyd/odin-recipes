Haddock gratin

Description

A cheesy, rich, fish dish

Ingredients

  • Smoked Haddock
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Spinach
  • Creme fraiche
  • Spring Onions
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Cheddar

Steps

  1. Wilt spinach, then use to line baking dish.
  2. Lay on top haddock and tuck in cherry tomatoes.
  3. Dod on creme fraiche and grate over cheddar.
  4. Bake in oven until golden and bubbling.
