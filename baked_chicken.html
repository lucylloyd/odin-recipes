Baked Chicken

Description

Chicken baked in a creamy sauce

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Condensed Chicken Soup
  • Creme Fraiche
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Brocolli

Steps

    On a high heat, brown the chicken, cut into chunks. Set aside in a baking dish. Lower the heat and fry the chopped inion and garlic. Boil the brocolli, chopped, until al dente. Throw the veg and chicken altogether in the baking dish, then mix in the creme fraiche and condensed soup. Top with breadcrumbs and parmesan. Bake at 180oC until browned and bubbling.
